Quick Answers

Frequently Asked

How long does shipping take within India?+
Orders are dispatched within 2–3 business days. Standard delivery across India takes 5–7 business days. Expedited shipping (2–3 days) is available for an additional charge at checkout.
Do you ship internationally?+
Yes, we ship to over 40 countries. International orders typically arrive within 7–14 business days. Customs duties and import taxes may apply depending on your country — these are the responsibility of the buyer.
What is your return policy?+
We offer a 15-day return policy on all items in original condition. Custom or personalised pieces cannot be returned. To initiate a return, email [email protected] with your order ID and reason for return.
Are your products truly handmade?+
Absolutely. Every product on Antarmahal is handcrafted by artisan families we work with directly. Each piece comes with a certificate of authenticity and an artisan story card — so you always know who made what you're wearing or displaying.
Can I commission a custom piece?+
Yes! We accept bespoke commissions for both apparel and sculptures. Turnaround time is typically 4–8 weeks depending on complexity. Get in touch via the form above or email us at [email protected] to discuss your vision.
